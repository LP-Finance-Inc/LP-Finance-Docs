# Division of CBS Protocol

**CBS Protocol can be divided into two subprotocols**

### CBS Protocol

* Supply Interest: 0%
* Borrow Interest: 0%
* CBS DAO: Not Applied
* Deposit Fee: 0.1%

### CBS Protocol v2

* Supply Interest: Variable (Depends on lending protocol rates)
* Borrow Interest: 0%
* CBS DAO: Applied
* Deposit Fee: 0.5%
